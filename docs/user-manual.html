Introduction

Habitat is a habit tracking app designed to help you build better routines and stay consistent over time. You can create habits, track your progress, reflect on your activity, and stay motivated through weekly summaries and social features.

This guide will walk you through how to use Habitat step by step. You can expand each feature below to learn what it does and how to use it in the app.

Get the App

To use Habitat, you’ll first need to install the app on your device.

View Installation Instructions

The full setup guide includes download links and step-by-step instructions for Android and iOS.

Quick Start

New to Habitat? Follow these simple steps to get started quickly.

  1. Install the app
    Download Habitat using the installation link on the home page.
  2. Create your first habit
    Tap “Add Habit” and either create one yourself or use AI suggestions.
  3. Check in daily
    Mark your habits as complete each day and optionally add notes or difficulty ratings.
  4. Track your progress
    Use the dashboard, calendar, and habit detail pages to stay consistent.
  5. Review your weekly recap
    Check your Habitat Wrapped to understand your patterns and progress.

Tip: Start with one or two habits and build consistency before adding more. Habit Stacking can help guide you through this process.

Feature Guide

Explore each feature below to learn how Habitat helps you build and track better habits.

Dashboard

The dashboard is your main home screen. It helps you navigate your habits and see your progress at a glance.

You can:

  • View all your habits in one place
  • See progress for each habit
  • Swipe through habit cards
  • Track your streaks and consistency
  • Add habits
  • View friends' lists
Create a Habit Manually

You can create habits based on your own goals and routines. This gives you full control over what you track and how often you do it.

You can set:

  • Habit name and description
  • Category (Fitness, Productivity, Wellness, etc.)
  • How often you want to do it (daily or weekly)
  • Whether the habit is public or private

Once created, your habit will appear on your dashboard and be ready to track.

Create a Habit with AI

If you’re not sure where to start, Habitat can suggest habits based on your goals.

How it works:

  • Enter a goal (for example: “Improve sleep”)
  • Get a list of suggested habits
  • Select the habit you want to add
  • Edit them before saving

This makes it easier to get started and discover habits that fit your lifestyle.

Calendar

The calendar shows your habit activity over time and helps you stay consistent.

You can:

  • View what habits were scheduled each day
  • See which habits were completed
  • Edit completed check-ins on past days to add notes or ratings
  • Check-in your habit for the day (and edit/remove them)

This is where you can go back and see your habit activity over time.

Habit Details

Each habit has its own detail page where you can see how well you’ve been doing over time.

You can:

  • See your current streak
  • View your completion rate
  • Look at a visual history of your check-ins
  • Check-in your habit for the day (and edit/remove them)
  • Edit the habit if needed

This helps you understand your consistency and track improvement.

Habit Stacking

Habit Stacking helps you avoid burnout by focusing on one habit at a time instead of trying to do everything at once. This feature is especially helpful if you're starting multiple habits at once.

How it works:

  • Rank your habits by priority
  • Only your top habit is active at first
  • Other habits stay inactive until you’re ready
  • You’ll get notified when it’s time to add the next habit
  • You can activate habits early if you want

This helps you build consistency step by step without feeling overwhelmed.

Habitat Wrapped

Habitat Wrapped gives you a weekly look into your habits and progress. It turns your check-ins, notes, difficulty ratings, and completion patterns into a more personal recap of your week.

Your Wrapped includes:

  • A personalized animal archetype that represents your habit style
  • Snapshot cards that highlight patterns in your week
    • A Completion Pulse showing how much you completed
    • A Category Leader showing your strongest habit area
    • A Rhythm Check showing your best and most challenging days
    • A Mood Board based on your difficulty ratings
  • An AI-generated summary of the last week based on your notes and activity

Wrapped helps you understand not just what you did, but how your habits felt and what patterns appeared throughout the week.

Friends

You can connect with friends to stay motivated and keep each other accountable.

You can:

  • Send and accept friend requests
  • View your friends list
  • See friends’ public habits and streaks

Your personal notes and ratings always remain private.

Profile

Your profile shows your personal progress in Habitat. It is where you can see your account information, earned points, and achievements.

You can:

  • View your name and account details
  • See your current point total
  • Track your level or progress status
  • View badges earned from habit milestones
  • Celebrate achievements such as streaks and consistent check-ins

You can earn points by completing habits and maintaining streaks. Badges help mark important milestones, such as building a multi-day streak or staying consistent over time.

Settings

Settings let you customize your Habitat experience and manage your account preferences.

You can:

  • Edit your display name, email, or timezone
  • Change your password
  • Adjust notification preferences
  • Turn habit stacking suggestions on or off
  • Switch between available app themes

Use settings to keep your account information updated and control how Habitat supports your routine.

FAQ and Troubleshooting

Why can’t I log in?

Double-check your email and password. If it still does not work, wait a few seconds and try again.

Why can’t I find the verification email?

Check your spam or junk folder. If you still can’t find it, try resending the verification email.

Why is my habit data not updating?

Try refreshing the screen, navigating away and back, or closing and reopening the app.

Why can’t I see a full Wrapped recap?

Wrapped needs enough habit activity from the week. Complete and log more habits to generate a fuller recap.

Why did my streak reset?

Missing a required habit check-in may reset your streak depending on the habit’s schedule.

Can I add check-ins for past days?

No. You can only edit existing check-ins by opening the calendar, choosing a past date, and updating your habit check-ins from there.

Can I edit a habit after creating it?

Yes. Open the habit’s detail page and use the edit option to update the habit.

Can I delete a habit?

Yes. Open the habit’s detail page and choose the delete option if you no longer want to track it.

Can I turn Habit Stacking off?

Yes. You can turn habit stacking suggestions on or off from Settings.

What are dormant habits?

Dormant habits are saved habits that are not active yet. They do not need check-ins until you activate them.

How do AI habit suggestions work?

You enter a goal, and Habitat suggests habits that may help you work toward it. You can choose and edit suggestions before saving.

Are my notes private?

Yes. Your daily notes and difficulty ratings stay private and are not shown to friends.

What can my friends see?

Friends can only see habits you marked as public, along with basic progress such as streaks.

How do I add a friend?

Open Friends, search for a user, and send a friend request. Once accepted, they will appear in your friends list.

How do I change my theme?

Open Settings and choose one of the available app themes.

How do I earn points?

You earn points by completing habits and maintaining streaks.

How do badges work?

Badges are earned by reaching habit milestones, such as maintaining streaks or completing habits consistently.

What should I do if the app freezes?

Close and reopen the app. If the issue continues, reinstall the app or contact the Habitat team.

Who should I contact if something is broken?

Contact the Habitat team and describe what happened, what screen you were on, and what you were trying to do.
